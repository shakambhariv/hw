About our school

St Xavier's College has been ranked 87th as per NIRF 2022 ranking. The college was awarded by UGC as t"College with a Potential for Excellence" award in 2006.The Chairman of The St. Xavier's Group, Dr. G.S Patnaik holds the office as the Managing Director. Being an acclaimed educationist of national repute, a caring benefactor and an able captain, he has been most successfully steering the Xaverianship and exploring all possibilities to spread education countrywide.



Campus life
Campus life

Saint Xavier University is a private institution that was founded in 1846. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 3,101 (fall 2021), its setting is city, and the campus size is 120 acres. It utilizes a semester-based academic calendar.

Achivements

Event date : 20th Dec, 2019 The 39th Indian National Cartographic Association’s International Congress on the theme “ New Age Cartography & Geospatial Technology in Digital India” was held from 18th to 20th December 2019 at Hotel Sun Park Inn Dehradun Uttarakhand. A team from Saint Xavier’s High School Patna took part in the 39th Inca National Map Quiz & stood 2nd at the National level. The team was guided by Dr. Shahla Kauser. The team comprised of:- 1- JAINENDRA NARAYAN SINGH, ‘ X C ’ 2- MAYANK ANAND, ‘ X C ’ 3- ABDUS SAMAD , ‘ X C ’ Jainendra Narayan Singh of class ‘ X C ’ represented the team at 39th Inca International Congress accompanied by Dr. Shahla Kauser, the concerned Geography teacher. The team awards was given by His Excellency Admiral General Vinay Badhwar, AVSM, PVSM, Chief Cartographer to the Government of India, Lieutenant General Girish Kumar, Suryeyor General of India & President INCA & Dr. V C Jha, Ex Vice - Chancellor, Bhagalpur University. JAINENDRA NARAYAN SINGH was the one and only student who got the golden opportunity to give vote of thanks at the event.
